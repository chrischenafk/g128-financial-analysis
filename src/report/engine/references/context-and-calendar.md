# Context layer: Seasonal calendar

## Explaining an anomaly
When an anomaly surfaces, consult the seasonal calendar below before marking it "unexplained."
Label external context as `Context (external, for verification):` in the report prose. Do not
invent causation if the calendar doesn't match; mark genuinely unexplained items as
**"unexplained — investigation target"** and let the reader decide.

One step deep: an event may explain a sales move; do not infer strategy or future behavior on
top of it.

single-country move anomalous.
